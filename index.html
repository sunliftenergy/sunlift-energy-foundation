Our mission

We reduce energy costs and improve disaster resilience.

We develop scalable clean energy programs that help vulnerable communities access affordable power, improve reliability during outages, and create long-term energy stability.

Lower Energy Bills

We help households and community sites cut utility costs through solar and energy-saving improvements.

Disaster Readiness

We support backup power and resilience planning for communities affected by severe weather and outages.

National Scale

We start with strong pilots and expand through partnerships with housing groups, nonprofits, and local leaders.
